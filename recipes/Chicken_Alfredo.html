Chicken Alfredo Pasta Recipe

Description

This Creamy Chicken Alfredo recipe is easy enough for beginner cooks. Total cook time 30 minutes (Prep time: 15 mins and Cook time: 15 mins). This recipe has 6 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup butter
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon of garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente then drain.
  2. In a seperate pan place your cubed chicken and cook until it is cooked through and no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  3. Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat. Add salt, pepper and garlic salt. Increase heat to medium.
  4. Mix in grated Romano and Parmesan Cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
  5. Add Cooked Pasta and Chicken to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated
  6. Serve Hot and Enjoy!
