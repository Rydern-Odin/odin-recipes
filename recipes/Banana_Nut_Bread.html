Banana Nut Bread Recipe

Description

This simple banana nut bread recipe is wonderful when served warm with butter. Total time for this Recipe 1 hour and 5 minutes(Prep time: 10 mins, Cook Time: 45 mins, Additional Time: 10 mins). This recipe yeilds 2 loafs of bread which is 16 servings.

Ingredients:

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup mashed bananas
  • 2 large eggs
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup chopped pecans
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉ (175℃). Grease and flour two 8x4-Inch loaf pans. Set aside.
  2. Sift together flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and baking soda. Stir in bananas, eggs, oil, pecans, and vanilla. Pour batter into the prepared pans.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 60 minutes.
  4. Cool on a wire rack for 10 minutes before removing from the pans.
  5. Slice and Enjoy
